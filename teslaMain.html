Tesla

Tesla Motors - a company producing electric vehicles and their components, based pioneer in this area (as well as a pioneer of space tourism industry and space commerce) Elon Musk. Among the investors listed company, Larry Page and Sergey Brin, the owners of the corporation Google. The main objective of the company - to make electric vehicles available to users an average price segment; reduce dependence on gasoline engines, and hence the exhaust gases. Elon Musk task - to prove that you can drive on electricity. Among the recent statements Mask - Tesla at the cost of 20 thousand dollars for a couple of years.

The update also limits the speed of the semi-autonomous mode autopilot Tesla and introduces a number of enhancements, including automatic deceleration when driving around a curve and keeping the car on its lane, even if erased markings.
