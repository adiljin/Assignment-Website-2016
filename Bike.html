Innovative Bike

Nowadays everything evolves, even the most seemingly,the usual things. A group of enthusiastic engineers, ignoring the opinion that “it is not necessary to reinvent the wheel”, has created a futuristic model,that to accelerate to maximum speed literally for three seconds.Italians are known worldwide for its pasta and bullfights, and now declared themselves as creators and innovative bicycle. Developers from TRED design studios presented their new invention BestiaNera Hybrid Bike – a bike that has a superb aggressive and sporty design and the stunning performance.One of the major advantages BestiaNera Hybrid Bike is the fact that the bike is almost entirely made of carbon fiber.

Using this synthetic composite material allowed to give body incredible strength and at the same time provide a low weight of design – only 9.8 kg.BestiaNera – «hybrid» in the best traditions. The key is that the bicycle will drive as human muscle power and an electric motor. It feeds the electric motor from the lithium-ion battery. Bike also uses regenerative braking system to allow return of the traction motor power, while operating in the generator mode. All this makes the trip on BestiaNera Hybrid Bike how awesome adventure. The manufacturer promises, that three presses on the pedal will achieve the speed of 30 km \ h. However, it will only be possible when using an electric motor.

When you first look at the bike a natural question arises: where are such large-scale things like the engine and the battery? Strictly speaking, engines in the bike just two, and they, along with the battery, “hidden” in lenticular rear wheel drive. Worry about the electronics is not, because the disk enclosure protects it from water, moisture, dust and mechanical damage. In design of the bike has been used an innovative system of «Anti-Vibration System», which, combined with a comfortable adjustable seat makes cycling more comfortable. The system dynamically combined with existing hydraulic brake system that allows vehicles to stop in an instant. It remains to add that the bike has a very attractive, albeit a specific design.
