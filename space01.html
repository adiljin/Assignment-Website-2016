Astronomers have discovered the process of merging of two black holes

Scientists have long been studying the very nature of space objects of unknown and recently discovered a mysterious phenomenon - the process of merging the two massive black holes. Naturally, such a process can not be seen by any telescope, but it is estimated could create a schematic of the process image.

Infrared space observatory WISE two black holes in the center of a distant galaxy have been discovered. These studies are two of the world were involved in the telescope, which also showed that this galaxy has an unusual twisted jet substance (jets).

Processing of the data received from the telescope, is still ongoing. This anomalous galaxy was designated WISE J233237.05-505643,5 and immediately interested astronomers their unusual behavior, experts say NASA. The first assumption was that in this galaxy is very rapid formation of stars. However, subsequent studies have shown that found a truly unique case last stage of merging black holes, says firstpost.com.

As astronomers say, in every large galaxy has a supermassive black hole. These holes are formed in two ways. The first is the absorption of matter from the immediate vicinity. The main feature of the second method is just the merger of two smaller black holes, supermassive hole in one.

The action of the full merger of black holes can be observed only in the last stages, when they are both a short distance from each other - a few light-years. Although their interaction begins when the distance between them a few thousand light-years.

Such a discovery is very important for modern astronomy. Astronomers for the first time will be able to study such a rare phenomenon, as the merger of two black holes in one. The final stage of this activity is to generate gravitational waves that scientists will be studying.
