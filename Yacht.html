Amazing concept of yacht that can soar over the waves

It may be that in the near future the guests of the Spanish island of Ibiza can see in the coastal waters the amazing object, by shape reminiscent the geometric shape tetrahedron (triangular pyramid). It looks like this vehicle is so unusual that it may be mistaken for an alien spaceship,wicth made an emergency landing in the MediterraneanBut in fact, this is just a concept yacht, that can rise above the waves. The designer originally designed the yacht so that she was able to “levitate” above the water,thanks to submersible the vertical rack which is attached to the hull . By the bottom of the rack is attached something like a torpedo with wings. When a boat gains enough speed,stand extends, and her body is lifted above the water. During the parking,stand is pulled into the boat and yacht lowered onto the water surface.

Of course, such a concept is somewhere on the verge of reality and science fiction. But as you know,to inventors dream not forbid.
